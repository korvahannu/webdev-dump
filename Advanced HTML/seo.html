Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Rules of Search Engine Optimization are listed below.

  1. Have relevant content on your page
  2. Use keywords in your content
  3. Make your site mobile-friendly
  4. Provide only accurate information (do not provide incorrect information)
  5. How fast your page loads affects your SEO ranking
  6. Page engagement - How long your users stay on your site?
  7. Page accessibility
  8. Custom 404 page
  9. Use proper metatags
  10. Use HTTPS
  11. Use proper headings: one h1, multiple h2-h6
  12. Avoid popups
